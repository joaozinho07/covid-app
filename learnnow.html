Home Page°

Have the latest health information at your fingertips with the official information app. This app displays the latest news, events, features and last minute updates on outbreaks!

Function 1

The first function of his Covid-App, he is authorized to give the numbers in real time of cured and deaths. Plus, it lets you know everything about a pandemic worldwide.

Function 2

The second function of your Covid-App is to show you in real time the active cases with the percentage of beds taken and the critical condition of patients from all over the world. What's more, Covid-App gives you closed cases and the number of people who have been cured.

Notifications°

You can stay on top of everything that happens in the world due to Covid-19, through Covid-App notifications.

Country list view and select the country°

You can access the list of countries and select a country that you want to see Covid-19 underreporting and infection data.

Voice command°

You can select a chosen country to be able to view the latest news.

Selected-Country°

After selecting a country you can browse the app and stay on top of everything that was caused by covid in that country. You will have access to the death rate and numbers of people recovered. You will know the percentage of beds that were taken by infected and so on.

News°

Stay on top of what's going on in the world. Covid-App will bring all the news in real time to you of what is going on in the world. Policies, reports, political decisions and more.
